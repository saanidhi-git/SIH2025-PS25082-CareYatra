Our Mission

CareYatra is a dedicated digital health record system designed to ensure fair and impartial healthcare access for Kerala's migrant population. By providing a comprehensive health record system, we support public health surveillance, prevent disease transmission, and align with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
